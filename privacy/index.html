Privacy

Privacy and information handling.

Endbricks collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries, manage consultations, and operate the site responsibly.

When you submit a consultation request, Endbricks may collect your name, work email address, company information, role, and the business context you choose to share. That information is used only to review the inquiry, respond to you, and determine the most relevant next step.

Consultation requests submitted through this website are processed by Formspree. Formspree receives the information you enter in the form in order to deliver the submission to Endbricks. Cloudflare Turnstile is also used on the form to reduce automated spam submissions. You can review Formspree’s and Cloudflare’s privacy information directly through their websites.

This site may also rely on hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers as needed to operate securely and reliably. Endbricks does not sell personal information and does not use inquiry details for unrelated marketing claims.

If you do not want to submit information through the website form, do not use the form.
