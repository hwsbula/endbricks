FAQ

Questions about fit, process, and implementation.

This page helps answer the questions most decision-makers ask before engaging an AI implementation partner.

How do we know where AI is actually worth applying?

Start by reviewing operational bottlenecks, repetitive work, internal information flow, and decision-heavy steps. The right use case usually has clear business value and manageable implementation complexity.

Do we need to replace our current systems?

Usually not. In most cases, AI works best when integrated into existing systems and workflows rather than forcing broad system replacement.

What if our processes are not fully standardized?

That is common. In some cases, process cleanup or redesign should happen before automation. Endbricks treats process quality as part of implementation readiness.

How do you approach privacy and governance?

Privacy, permissions, model choice, review checkpoints, and operating controls should be built into the implementation plan from the outset.

Do you only provide strategy, or do you also implement?

Endbricks is positioned around both. Strategy matters, but the goal is to move from recommendation to working solution and practical adoption.

How long does a typical engagement take?

That depends on scope. A focused assessment may take days or weeks, while implementation timelines depend on workflow complexity, integration needs, and organizational readiness.
